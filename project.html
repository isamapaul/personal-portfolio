PAU Cafeteria Management System

Problem

Students often experienced long queues and delays when ordering food on campus.

Solution

I developed a cafeteria management system that allows users to place food orders digitally while staff manage orders efficiently.

Features

  • Online food ordering
  • Admin dashboard
  • Order tracking

Technologies Used

Java, MySQL, HTML, CSS

What I Learned

I learned how to structure a full application, manage data, and design user-friendly interfaces.
